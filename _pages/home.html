Tanisha Ghorpade, Founder

“Team Green primarily works to eradicate any stigma associated with reusing clothes. Secondhand clothing or as we call it, sustainable fashion is for anyone who wants to appreciate and enjoy fashion in harmony with nature. Through our circular consumerism model, we magnify the social and environmental benefits of pre-owned fashion.

We are also actively involved in strengthening social capital within Pune city through community engagement amongst the youth. Furthermore, recognizing the growing economic disparity within our modern society, we work to facilitate a transfer of wealth and resources to the underprivileged.”

Your wardrobe is full of possibilities

Worldwide 73% of all our clothes end up in landfills. Clothes can take upto 40 years to decompose and shoes can take upto 1000 years to breakdown. It also takes 2700 litres of water to make a cotton shirt and 6300 litres to make a pair of jeans. Greening your closet can make a world of an impact.

Play your part in a green fashion and lifestyle revolution. Our platform provides an avenue for conscious consumption. We have an eye for curating great preloved styles and offer a variety of everyday eco-friendly products. We’re here to help you live more sustainably and to help you do your part in making the world a better place.
