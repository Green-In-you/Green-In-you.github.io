Tanisha Ghorpade

Founder & President

“I am a part of the generation which is living their prime years in the midst of a climate crisis. A generation that's bearing the brunt of rising mental illnesses and is also subject to a chillingly uncertain future. Green In You was a call to action born from an intense desire to be in the driver's seat for creating a better tomorrow both socially and environmentally. Green In You has personally equipped me with tremendous confidence. It empowered me to believe that when people come together for the common good, anything is possible! Our conscious quest to make a real difference in the world will forever continue, and I hope you can support us on our relentless journey in service to people and the planet.”

Divya Das

Head of Human Resources

Ritvik Bhardwaj

Green in You 2019 was a huge success and I’m sure with the current team and our efforts, Green in You’s future events will be much bigger and much better. I believe together we can achieve more. Cannot wait to host y’all yet again!”
