Vision

To facilitate sustainable consumerism in urban India

Mission

We aim to create a community which enables socializing, networking, learning, developing professionally and most importantly, empowering individuals to lead sustainable lifestyles. As we work towards our primary mission, we also seek to bridge inter- and intra-generational financial, material and intellectual resource gaps within our community.

However, today, people’s shopping habits have extensively changed because of stimulus from the fashion giants. Since companies churn out brand new styles about 52 times a year people feel “out-of-fashion” quickly and desire new clothing. Therefore, unlike previous times, clothing gets discarded not because it is ragged, but simply because people are bored of it.”

If these barely used clothes are still in prime condition, they really should not be thrown away and swamp the planet, causing catastrophic environment damage. Instead, we should work to get rid of the stigma present and normalise reusing clothes. Sustainable fashion is not just for the low socio-economic class; it is for every conscious individual who wants to appreciate and enjoy fashion in harmony with nature!”

OUR IMPACTS

Through setting up Green In You, we were able to encourage people from a strata of the society who never in their lives had shopped secondhand. They now enthusiastically come out to normalize reusing clothes. We built a respectable environment which destigmatized secondhand shopping and sowed a seed for fighting against the second largest polluter in the world, fashion!

Our circular model extends the lifespan of thousands of products per sale, keeping resources within the economy and hence diverting them from landfills. GIY works to reduce the demand for virgin material resources and their consequent extractive impacts.

We understand that as the ravaging effects of climate change are becoming more distinct, their brunt is unjustly borne by the poor. We see it as our responsibility to funnel economic, intellectual and material resources towards the lower socio-economic classes in order to build their capacity to prosper.

The youth coming together to set up our events are involved in exploring their creative energies, collaborating effectively and developing their professional acumen. At Green In You, we believe that to solve problems bigger than ourselves it is essential to actively engage within the local community and cultivate a sense of purpose amongst us. Strengthening our social capital helps us to make our world a better place.

Let’s work together!

Our team is dedicated to empower urban India to lead sustainable lifestyles. We want to make the world a greener and better place to live in for everyone.

We're always looking for individuals as well as organizations who believe in our vision and want to work with us towards the common good.

Sustainable fashion is not just for the lower socio- economic class, it is for every conscious individual who wants to appreciate and enjoy fashion in harmony with nature!
